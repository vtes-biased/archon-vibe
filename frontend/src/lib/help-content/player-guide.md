# Player Guide

Welcome to Archon, the official VEKN tournament management app. This guide covers everything you need as a player.

## Finding Tournaments

Browse the **Tournaments** page to see upcoming events. You can filter by country, format (Constructed, Limited), and date. Use **My Agenda** to see tournaments you're registered for.

Subscribe to the calendar feed to get tournament dates in your calendar app.

## Registering for a Tournament

Once registration is open, click **Register** on the tournament page. You'll see your status change to "Registered."

If the organizer requires decklists, upload your deck before check-in to avoid a warning sanction.

## Checking In

When you arrive at the venue, check in using one of these methods:

- **QR Code**: Tap "Scan QR to Check In" and point your camera at the organizer's QR code displayed at the venue.
- **Self check-in**: If available, tap "Check In" on the tournament page.
- **Organizer check-in**: The organizer can check you in from their player list.

## Uploading Your Decklist

Go to the tournament page and find the deck section. You can upload your deck by:

- **Pasting** the deck list text directly
- **From URL**: Paste a link from VDB (vdb.im), VTESDecks, or Amaranth
- **QR code**: Scan a VDB deck QR code

You can replace your deck until the tournament starts.

## During the Tournament

After each round starts, check the **Rounds** tab to see your table assignment. After your table finishes, the organizer will enter the results.

## Understanding Your Ratings

After the tournament finishes, your rating points are calculated based on your performance. Check the **Rankings** page to see your position. Ratings are tracked separately for:

- Constructed
- Constructed Online
- Limited
- Limited Online
